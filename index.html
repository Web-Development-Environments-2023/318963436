    About me

    Hapoel Tel Aviv is a professional football team based in Tel Aviv, Israel. The team was founded in 1923 and has a long history of success in Israeli football. Hapoel Tel Aviv has won 13 championships and 16 cups in Israeli football, making it one of the most successful clubs in the country. The team plays its home matches at the Bloomfield Stadium, which has a capacity of 29,150 spectators. Hapoel Tel Aviv has a large and passionate fan base, with supporters known for their fervent and vocal support of the team. Hapoel Tel Aviv is a storied football club with a rich history and passionate fan base in Israeli football.
